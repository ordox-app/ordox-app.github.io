Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2025

1. Data Collection and Usage

Ordox collects and processes the following information locally on your device:

  • App usage statistics (time spent in apps)
  • List of installed applications
  • App launch frequencies
  • Your self-defined app usage limits

2. Local Data Processing

All data processing occurs exclusively on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Transfer data to external servers
  • Share your usage data with third parties
  • Store data in cloud services

3. Required Permissions

Our app requires the following Android permissions to function:

  • Usage Access Permission: To monitor app usage time and frequency
  • Display Over Other Apps Permission: To show blocking screens when usage limits are reached
  • Battery Optimization Permission: To ensure proper background monitoring

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This includes:

  • App usage statistics
  • Your custom app limits and restrictions
  • Pro subscription status

5. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat for processing in-app purchases. Payment processing is handled securely by RevenueCat and Google Play Store. We do not store payment information.

6. Data Deletion

All app data is automatically removed when you uninstall the app. No manual deletion process is required as no data is stored on external servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not specifically target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at dekar.rene@gmail.com
