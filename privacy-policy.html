    <h1>Privacy Policy</h1>

    <p class="last-updated">Last updated: February 8, 2025</p>


    <h2>1. Data Collection and Usage</h2>

    <p>Ordox collects and processes the following information locally on your device:</p>

    <ul>

        <li>App usage statistics (time spent in apps)</li>

        <li>List of installed applications</li>

        <li>App launch frequencies</li>

        <li>Your self-defined app usage limits</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>2. Local Data Processing</h2>

    <p>All data processing occurs exclusively on your device. We do not:</p>

    <ul>

        <li>Collect personal information</li>

        <li>Transfer data to external servers</li>

        <li>Share your usage data with third parties</li>

        <li>Store data in cloud services</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>3. Required Permissions</h2>

    <p>Our app requires the following Android permissions to function:</p>

    <ul>

        <li>Usage Access Permission: To monitor app usage time and frequency</li>

        <li>Display Over Other Apps Permission: To show blocking screens when usage limits are reached</li>

        <li>Battery Optimization Permission: To ensure proper background monitoring</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>4. Data Storage</h2>

    <p>All data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This includes:</p>

    <ul>

        <li>App usage statistics</li>

        <li>Your custom app limits and restrictions</li>

        <li>Pro subscription status</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>5. Third-Party Services</h2>

    <p>We use RevenueCat for processing in-app purchases. Payment processing is handled securely by RevenueCat and Google Play Store. We do not store payment information.</p>


    <h2>6. Data Deletion</h2>

    <p>All app data is automatically removed when you uninstall the app. No manual deletion process is required as no data is stored on external servers.</p>


    <h2>7. Children's Privacy</h2>

    <p>Our app does not specifically target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.</p>


    <h2>8. Changes to This Policy</h2>

    <p>We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.</p>


    <h2>9. Contact Us</h2>
